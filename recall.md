## Expectation

$N_{XY}$: the number of customers who bought both item X and item Y.

$E_{XY}$: the expected num of customers who bought both X and Y.

$$ E_{XY} = |X buyers|*P(Y) $$
$$ P(Y) = \frac{|Y \quad buyers|}{|all\quad buyers|} $$
